## Sizing Quick Reference

When a customer tells you about their shop, here's how to match them to a compressor:

| Shop Description | Typical CFM Need | Recommended Compressor |
|-----------------|------------------|----------------------|
| 1-2 CNC machines, small job shop | 15-25 CFM | JRS-5E (5 HP) |
| 3-5 CNC machines, general manufacturing | 25-50 CFM | JRS-75E or JRS-10E (7.5-10 HP) |
| 5-10 machines, busy production floor | 50-100 CFM | JRS-15E or JRS-25PRO (15-25 HP) |
| 10+ machines, multi-shift operation | 100-200 CFM | JRS-30PRO or JVSD-50 (30-50 HP) |
| Full plant, 200+ CFM continuous | 200-500 CFM | JVSD-75 to JVSD-125 (75-125 HP) |
| Large manufacturing facility | 500+ CFM | JVSD-150, JVSD-200, or JLF series |

**Rule of thumb:** A typical CNC machine needs 8-15 CFM. A paint booth needs 15-30 CFM. A sandblaster needs 50-100 CFM.

**Always oversize by 20-30%.** If their math says 80 CFM, recommend a 100 CFM unit. Undersizing kills the compressor and kills the customer relationship.
